Frequently Asked Questions

How often should cattle be vaccinated?

Vaccination schedules depend on age and health, but generally, annual boosters are recommended.

What should I do if my cow is sick?

Contact a veterinarian immediately. Early intervention is crucial for recovery.

How can I support cattle health at home?

Provide clean water, balanced nutrition, regular deworming, and a clean environment.
